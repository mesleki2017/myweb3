What is HomePro?

HomePro is a home care platform that connects homeowners with professional service providers offering a wide range of home services, including repairs, maintenance, cleaning, and more.

Are the service providers on HomePro reliable and qualified?

Yes! At HomePro, we carefully vet every service provider before they join our platform. Each professional undergoes a strict verification process, which includes checking licenses, experience, and customer reviews. We only accept qualified and reputable experts to ensure you receive top-quality service. Additionally, our rating and review system allows users to share their experiences, helping others make informed decisions.

What if I have an issue or complaint about a service provider?

If you encounter any issues with a service provider, HomePro is here to help. You can report your concern through our customer support team, who will review the case and take appropriate action. Depending on the situation, we may offer a refund, mediation, or assistance in finding a replacement professional. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we strive to maintain a high standard of service.

How are payments handled on HomePro?

Payments on HomePro are secure and straightforward. Once you book a service, you can pay directly through our platform using various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. The payment is held securely until the job is completed to your satisfaction. This ensures that both customers and service providers have a safe and transparent transaction process.

How do I leave a review for a service provider?

Leaving a review is simple! After your service is completed, you’ll receive an email or app notification asking for feedback. You can rate your experience and leave a detailed review on the provider’s profile. Reviews help other customers make informed choices and allow us to maintain high service standards. Your feedback is valuable and helps improve the HomePro community!
